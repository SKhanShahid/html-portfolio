The Best Movies According to Me.

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Interstellar

A visually stunning space epic that explores time, love, and survival in the far reaches of the universe..

3 Idiots

A classic comedy-drama that explores the pressures of the Indian education system with a strong message about following your passion.

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara

A heartfelt story of friendship, self-discovery, and adventure set against the backdrop of a road trip in Spain.
